ABOUT ME

Engineer. Builder. Founder.

“I don’t build for portfolios.
I build things that actually run.”

Satyam Kumar is a 3rd year B.Tech CSE student specialising in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning at Lovely Professional University (LPU), Phagwara, Punjab.

He is the co-founder of Courage Library — a live production SaaS platform for exam preparation (SSC, Banking, Railway, Defence) with real users, Razorpay payments, and Supabase backend. Not a tutorial. A real product.

Satyam built Medlyf — a 10-minute medicine delivery PWA with prescription verification and partner dashboards — and Safe Text Monitor, a complete ML pipeline for toxic content classification using NLP and TF-IDF on the Kaggle Jigsaw dataset.

As Hostel President at LPU, he led student governance at scale. He has attended open-source conferences at IIT Kanpur and IIT Ropar. Primary stack: Python, Flask, React, JavaScript.
